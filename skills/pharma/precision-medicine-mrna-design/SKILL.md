# Precision Medicine & mRNA Design

## Overview

Provides a framework for neoantigen identification and mRNA construct design for personalized veterinary cancer therapy.

## When to Use

- Designing personalized immunotherapy
- Analyzing genomic tumor data for neoantigens
- Keywords: mRNA, vaccine, neoantigen, AlphaFold, LNP, Rosie

## Workflow

1. **Genomic Analysis:** Identify tumor-specific mutations vs. healthy tissue.
2. **Neoantigen Prediction:** Use LLMs to brainstorm targets and AlphaFold to predict 3D protein binding.
3. **Sequence Design:** Construct the mRNA sequence (5' cap, ORF, Poly-A tail).
4. **Delivery:** Formulate for Lipid Nanoparticle (LNP) injection.

## Limitations

- Experimental; requires specialist oncology and bioinformatics oversight.
- Regulatory approval varies by jurisdiction.
