# Comparative Oncology & One Health

## Overview

Accelerates therapy discovery by mapping translational endpoints between canine clinical trials and human medicine.

## When to Use

- Cross-referencing veterinary and human oncology trials
- Evaluating translational endpoints for immunotherapy
- Keywords: comparative oncology, One Health, canine cancer model, translational, mRNA-ZOX40L, Tufts, co-discovery

## Workflow

1. **Cross-Species Mapping:** Compare canine trial results (e.g., Tufts mRNA-ZOX40L) with human Phase 1 data.
2. **Signal Detection:** Monitor for shared adverse events (e.g., cytokine release syndrome).

## Limitations

- Extrapolation requires biological plausibility verification.
